My Services

Owambe

Owambe is a party, Nigerian style. A celebration of life, an Owambe usually marks a milestone – a baby naming, wedding, and I guarantee you the best look to any Nigerian party.

Bridal

Bridal makeup is a type of makeup that's applied on a bride's wedding day to enhance her natural beauty. It's usually more complex and precise than regular makeup, and is meant to last the entire day.

Photoshoot

Models often wear more makeup for photoshoots than they would in everyday life. This is because the camera and lighting can wash out the skin's natural color and features. Heavier makeup helps to add definition and color back to the face, making it stand out in photographs.
